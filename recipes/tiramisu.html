Tiramisu recipe

Description

There are many, many Tiramisu recipes out there in this big wide world. Some are complicated. Some are very shortcut, made using cream in a can.

Ingredients

  • Mascarpone
  • Ladyfingers
  • Coffee
  • Kahlua
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Separate egg yolks and whites
  2. Beat them separately with sugar
  3. Mix with mascarpone
  4. Soak ladyfingers in coffee and kahlua
  5. Layer
  6. Eat
